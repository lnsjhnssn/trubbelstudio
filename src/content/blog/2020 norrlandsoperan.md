From 2016 to the end of 2019, I was employed at Norrlandsoperan in Umeå. It is a multidisciplinary venue featuring opera, dance, symphonic concerts, and contemporary art, with four stages and two of them with up to 500 seats.

During my time there, I worked with Swedish and international companies, taking part in sound implementation, live mixing, technical planning, and company hosting. The role allowed me to combine hands-on technical work with production coordination, supporting diverse performances ranging from contemporary opera to experimental dance.
