**Role:** Sound Artist, Co-Creator  
**Exhibition:** Under marken, Galleri Syster, Luleå, Sweden  
**Dates:** 19 March – 11 April 2021

I collaborated with **artist Helena Wikström** and **writer Andrea Lundgren** on _under marken_, an immersive, multidisciplinary installation presented at **Galleri Syster** as part of their **SamArtBete** project series.

_under marken_,, a subterranean world of interconnected chambers filled with sculpture, video, text, and **layered soundscapes**. The work evoked a complex underground network of memory, instinct, and transformation, where multiple voices whispered, sang, or echoed through sculpted roots and shifting walls.

My role focused on the **creation of the sound environment**. A polyphonic audio layer that blended composed elements, field recordings, voice, and ambient textures. The sound was woven throughout the space as both a narrative and sculptural element, contributing to the piece’s dreamlike, immersive quality.

Built collaboratively from scratch, the installation played with themes of **collective identity**, **buried memory**, and the fragile boundary between the natural and human-made. It invited visitors to wander, discover, and interpret freely—some rooms hidden, others overgrown, and some accessible only by sound.
