**Role:** Dialogue Editing, Sound Mixing

I was responsible for the **dialogue editing** and **final mix** of the short film **Parlez-moi des amours**, which was screened as part of the **Agora Les Amours** intergenerational festival at **Théâtre Am Stram Gram** (Geneva) in May 2024.

The film explores the emotional complexity of love through the voices of young people, in conversation with psychotherapist **Isabelle Filliozat** and psychiatrist **Vincent Girard**. Tackling topics such as affection, heartbreak, and relationships across generations, the film offers a candid and tender look into what it means to love at 10, 20, or 50 years old.
