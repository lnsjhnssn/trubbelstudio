**Role:** Sound Design, Dialogue Editing, Sound Mixing  
**Director:** Orélie Fuchs Chen  
**Duration:** 36 min | Digital & Super 8 | Format 4:3  
**Awards:** Grand Prix – 75e Biennale d’art contemporain de La Chaux-de-Fonds (2024)

_Je vous suis_, is the first medium-length film by Swiss artist and theatre director **Orélie Fuchs Chen**. The film brings together nine characters—some with visible or invisible disabilities — exploring their identities, vulnerabilities, and relationships in a narrative that resists classification. Sound played a vital role in expressing the physicality and fragility of presence and supporting the film’s hybrid language between theatre, poetry, and cinema.

The film was awarded the **Grand Prix of the 75th Biennale of Contemporary Art** in La Chaux-de-Fonds in January 2024.
