**Role:** Web Design, Web Development, UI, CMS Integration

Built with: Astro + TinaCMS

Designed and developed the official website for **Atelier Tentatif**, a theatre company that creates **site-specific performances inspired by nature**, aimed at engaging both children and adults in reflecting on our relationship with the natural world.

The site was built using **Astro**, a modern, fast static site framework, combined with **TinaCMS** to allow for easy, real-time content editing.

Content was carefully structured to highlight the accessibility and mobility of their shows, which are designed to be performed **indoors or outdoors**, often in non-traditional spaces. The aesthetic of the site draws from natural imagery and simplicity, echoing the company’s values of experimentation, ecology, and community outreach.

Atelier Tentatif is a long-standing collaboration between **Renato Campora**, **Stefania Nuzzo**, and **Olivier Mäusli**, with roots in the Canton of Vaud and a strong presence in Geneva. The new website supports both the **Atelier Tentatif Genève** and **Atelier Tentatif Vaud** branches, and serves as a platform for showcasing their creations, communicating with venues and schools, and promoting upcoming events.

Visit the site: [ateliertentatif.ch](http://ateliertentatif.ch)
