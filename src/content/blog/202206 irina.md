**Role:** Live Sound Engineer  
**Production:** Cie Point de Suture / Marika Dreistadt

From 2021 to 2023, I worked as **sound engineer** on the documentary theatre production **IRINA**, directed by **Marika Dreistadt**. The piece explores the story of Irina Bialek through a deeply personal and multi-layered theatrical form, blending **live performance**, **literature**, and **radiophonic fiction**.

I was responsible for the **live sound operation**, supporting the **original sound design by Jérémie Conne**.

Performances: 2021–2023  
Sound Design: Jérémie Conne  
Directed by: Marika Dreistadt
