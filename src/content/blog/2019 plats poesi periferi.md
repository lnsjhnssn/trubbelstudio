Filmmaker **Jonas Börjesson**, from Halmstad, followed the poetry residency **Place. Poetry. Periphery.**, creating a poetic film inspired by its themes. The film features poets Jun Feng / Jimbut, Åsa Maria Kraft, Morten Langeland, Jonas Rolsted, Heidi von Wright, and Helena Österlund.

The residency, organized by **Art Inside Out** in Hylte Municipality (Halland, Sweden), brought together six Nordic poets to explore how contemporary poetry can reflect rural life and spark social change. Set in a landscape shaped by forestry and farming, the project questioned how place, nature, and poetry intersect.

I created the music for the film as part of **Old Amica**, and we were honored to be nominated for **Best Music Score** at the Annual Copenhagen Film Festival 2018.
