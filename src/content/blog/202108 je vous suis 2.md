**Role:** Sound Engineering and Design together with Orélie Fuchs
<br>
**Artist:** Orélie Fuchs Chen  
**Venue:** Laténium – Parc et musée d’archéologie de Hauterive  
**Year:** 2021

_Je vous suis / 2e_, a poetic and inclusive performance project directed by **Orélie Fuchs Chen** and co-created with **Kilian Haselbeck** and the **Cie dansehabile**. The project brought together **eight performers** for a two-week immersive residency and creation process at the museum **Laténium** in Neuchâtel.

This second chapter of _Je vous suis_ explored **the limits of life, the body, and disability**, drawing on the performers' lived experiences and creative gestures. My role involved creating a **sound environment** that supported the layered emotional and physical landscapes of the piece. Blending textures, live movement, and poetic resonance into the museum and its natural surroundings.

Concept & Direction: Orélie Fuchs Chen  
Co-Creation: Kilian Haselbeck & Cie dansehabile  
Sound Design: Linus Johansson & Orélie Fuchs Chen
Venue: Laténium, Neuchâtel
