Je vous suis / 2e, a poetic and inclusive performance project directed by Orélie Fuchs Chen and co-created with Kilian Haselbeck and the Cie dansehabile. The project brought together eight performers for a two-week immersive residency and creation process at the museum Laténium in Neuchâtel.

This second chapter of Je vous suis explored the limits of life, the body, and disability\*\*, drawing on the performers' lived experiences and creative gestures. My role involved creating a sound environment that supported the layered emotional and physical landscapes of the piece. Blending textures, live movement, and poetic resonance into the museum and its natural surroundings.

**Concept & Direction:** Orélie Fuchs Chen. **Co-Creation:** Kilian Haselbeck & Cie dansehabile **Sound Design:** Linus Johansson & Orélie Fuchs Chen. **Venue:** Laténium, Neuchâtel.
