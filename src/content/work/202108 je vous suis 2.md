Je vous suis / 2e, a performance project directed by Orélie Fuchs Chen and co-created with Kilian Haselbeck and the Cie dansehabile. The project brought together eight performers for a two-week immersive residency and creation process at the museum Laténium in Neuchâtel.

Concept & Direction: Orélie Fuchs Chen. Co-Creation: Kilian Haselbeck & Cie dansehabile Sound Design: Linus Johansson & Orélie Fuchs Chen. Venue: Laténium, Neuchâtel.
