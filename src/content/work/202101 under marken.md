I collaborated with artist Helena Wikström and writer Andrea Lundgren on Under marken, a multidisciplinary installation presented at Galleri Syster as part of their SamArtBete project series.

Built collaboratively from scratch, the installation played with themes of collective identity, buried memory, and the boundary between the natural and human-made. It invited visitors to wander, discover, and interpret freely. Some rooms hidden, others overgrown, and some accessible only by listening.
